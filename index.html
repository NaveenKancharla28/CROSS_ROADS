Menu

A curated journey across India—from Kashmir’s aromatic valleys to Kerala’s coastal spice routes. At CROSS ROADS, every dish is crafted with regional authenticity, origin-sourced spices, and a fine-dining touch that elevates India’s timeless culinary heritage.

Lunch

  • Kashmiri Rogan Josh • slow-cooked lamb, aromatic red chili
  • Delhi Street Chaat Platter • dahi puri, papdi chaat, tangy chutneys
  • South Indian Thali • sambar, rasam, poriyal, curd rice

Dinner

  • Hyderabadi Dum Biryani • saffron rice, hand-ground spice blend
  • Goan Prawn Curry • kokum, coconut, coastal spice infusion
  • Paneer Tikka Masala • charred cottage cheese, creamy tomato fenugreek sauce

Desserts & Wine

  • Gulab Jamun Cheesecake • rose syrup, pistachio crumble
  • Kerala Elaneer Pudding • tender coconut, cardamom
  • Premium Indian Liquors • Amrut, Paul John, artisanal spirits
